About the company

Every successful sporting career requires talent, willpower plus professional, efficient career planning. The master sports team offers services going far beyond those of a mere agency. As well as acting as agents, we also provide advice, look after players and coordinate their schedules, and, on request, we even take care of seemingly unrelated matters such as filing and managing important documents… Master sports has specially dedicated itself to working with young, talented players. We supply everything required for ensuring our young clients receive an effective career planning service specially tailored to their own particular needs.
